One of the bands I play in is the Rowland Hall jazz band. We perform four times a year, usually in front of the school and parents. The band is made up of 12, 13, and 14 year olds, with a new band starting in high school.

The other band I play for - outside of school - is the Caleb Chapman jazz band. We've traveled and toured all over Utah at many different venues and places. The band is very hard to get into and very prestigious around Utah, being one of the best bands in Utah.
