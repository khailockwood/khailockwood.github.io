I've played basketball for 8 years and played for 6 teams. I play shooting guard, small forward and sometimes point guard if needed. I'm a lefty and watch players like James Harden or Manu Ginobili and try and imitate them. I practice every day and work hard to get better.

The current team I'm on is Utah Elite. I joined them in September 2016, first joining the B lower division team them moving up to the A team. We've been to tournaments around the country, ranging from Houston to Virginia to Las Vegas or California. We've won state championships multiple times and placed as high as 8th in nationals.
